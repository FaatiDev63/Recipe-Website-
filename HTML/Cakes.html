Classic Chocolate Cake

Classic Chocolate Cake
Ingredients
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1/2 cup hot water
  • 1 cup castor sugar
  • 1 cup flour
  • 4 eggs (separated)
  • 2 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoons vanilla essence
Method 1
Place cocoa, oil and hot water into a mixing bowl and stir well. Then add castor sugar, flour, slightly beaten egg yolk, 2 teaspoons baking powder and vanilla essence and mix well. Beat the egg whites with half teaspoon baking powder until stiff and fold into the first mixture. Pour mixture into two greased round cake tins or one cake tin measuring approximately 28 x 21cm. Bake for 20 - 25 minutes in a moderate oven.

Method 2
Place cocoa, oil, hot water, castor sugar, flour, egg yolks, 2 teaspoons baking powder and vanilla essence into a bowl and beat well together. Beat egg whites with half teaspoon baking powder until stiff and fold into first mixture. Put into a greased baking pan and bake in a moderate oven for 20 - 25 minutes.

Can be decorated with chocolate ganache, whipped cream, fruit or anything that tickles your fancy. For more ideas and frosting recipes go to www.epicurious.com

Carrot Cake

Carrot Cake
Ingredients
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup castor sugar
  • 1 cup oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 2 cups carrots, finely grated
  • 1/2 cup pecan nuts, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon powder
  • pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups cake flour
  • 3 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of bicarbonate of soda
  • 1/4 cup water
Method
Beat the eggs and sugar until creamy. Add the oil and vanilla, beat well. Add the carrots, pecans, cinnamon and salt and mix well. Fold in the dry ingredients alternately with the water, mix until well combined. Pour mixture into 2 well-greased round 20cm cake tins and bake in a preheated oven at 180 degrees for 30 minutes. Cool completely and decorate with cream cheese frosting and pecans.

Cream Cheese Frosting
Ingredients
  • 1 cup fresh cream
  • 1 cup icing sugar, sifted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla essence
  • 250 grams cream cheese

Method
Beat the fresh cream, icing sugar and vanilla on high speed until peaks are formed, then set aside. In a separte bowl beat the cream cheese until smooth. Add the cream cheese to the fresh cream and fold lightly until well combined, do not over-mix. Refrigerate before using.
