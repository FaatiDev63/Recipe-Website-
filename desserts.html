No Bake Cheesecake

No Bake Cheesecake
Ingredients
  • 1 can (385 g) condense milk
  • 1 can (290 g) dessert cream
  • 1 cup coffee creamer
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 125 g melted butter
  • 1 packet tennis biscuits
Method
Crush the biscuits until fine. Add the butter and mix until combined. Spoon into prepared spring form tin and press firmly onto base. Refrigerate for 20 minutes or until firm. Combine the condense milk, dessert cream, creamer and lemon juice and mix well with a whisk or electric beater. Pour over the biscuit base and chill well for 4-6 hours or preferably overnight.
Decorate with whipped cream and seasonal fruit or serve with your desired toppings.

Alternatively make a flavoured cheescake with a layer of jelly or a fruit compote. For various compote recipes go to www.bbcgoodfood.com
